Why Your Giving Matters

At Praise Congregation Oyibi, we believe in the power of giving to further God's kingdom. Your tithes, offerings, and donations are foundational to everything we do. They enable us to:

  • Maintain Church Operations: Covering essential costs like utility bills, building maintenance, and administrative expenses.
  • Fund Ministries: Supporting our vibrant Children's Ministry, Youth Fellowship, Women's Ministry, and other vital church groups.
  • Community Outreach: Investing in evangelism, compassionate initiatives, and programs that directly benefit the Oyibi community and beyond.
  • Media Ministry: Helping The Media Team acquire and maintain equipment, software, and training to ensure high-quality broadcasts and digital content.

Every contribution, no matter the size, makes a significant difference and is a testament to your partnership in God's work.
