About The Media Team

Learn about our mission, vision, and the dedicated individuals who serve.

Our Story

The Media Team at Praise Congregation Oyibi began with a humble vision: to use technology as a powerful tool for spreading the Gospel and enhancing the worship experience. From our early days with basic sound equipment, we have grown, driven by a passion to ensure that every sermon, every song, and every announcement reaches the congregation clearly and effectively, both within the church walls and beyond.

Over the years, we've embraced new technologies, from live streaming services to producing engaging digital content for social media. Our journey is one of continuous learning and adaptation, always with the goal of serving the church's mission to connect hearts and share God's Word in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To leverage modern media and technology to support all ministries of Praise Congregation Oyibi, enabling the effective dissemination of the Gospel, enhancing the worship experience, and fostering vibrant community engagement.

Our Vision

To be a leading example of media ministry, inspiring congregations across Ghana and beyond to embrace digital tools for spiritual growth, outreach, and transformation, making the Word of God accessible to all.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest quality in all our media productions and technical support.
  • Service: Dedicated to supporting the church's ministries with humility and a servant's heart.
  • Innovation: Embracing new technologies and creative approaches to ministry.
  • Teamwork: Collaborating effectively, valuing each member's contribution and gift.
  • Integrity: Upholding Christian principles in all our work and interactions.
