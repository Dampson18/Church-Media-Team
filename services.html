Our Services

Supporting the church's mission through dedicated media and technical expertise.

What We Do

The Media Team is at the heart of bringing the message of Praise Congregation Oyibi to life, both within our sanctuary and across digital platforms. We leverage technology and creativity to enhance worship, facilitate communication, and broaden our outreach.

Live Streaming & Online Broadcast

Ensuring our Sunday services, special programs, and events are broadcast live to our online congregation via YouTube, Facebook, and other platforms, making worship accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Audio & Sound Management

Providing professional sound reinforcement for all church gatherings, including sermons, praise and worship, and special performances, guaranteeing clear and balanced audio for all listeners.

Video Production & Editing

From sermon recordings to promotional videos for church events and testimonies, we capture and edit high-quality video content for various church needs and platforms.

Graphic Design & Visuals

Creating compelling visual content, including sermon slides, event flyers, social media graphics, and website banners, to communicate messages effectively and beautifully.

Website & Digital Platform Management

Maintaining and updating the church website and other digital platforms, ensuring timely information, accessible resources, and a smooth online experience for visitors.

Social Media Engagement

Managing the church's social media presence to share updates, inspiring content, event reminders, and fostering interaction within our online community.

How We Serve You

For Sunday Services

We handle all technical aspects of worship, including audio mixing, projection of lyrics and scripture, camera operation for live streams, and managing online platforms.

For Ministry Events

Partnering with various church ministries (e.g., Youth, Women's Fellowship) to provide media support for their programs, workshops, and outreaches, ensuring their messages are amplified.

For Community Outreach

Utilizing digital media to share the Gospel and church activities with the wider Oyibi community and beyond, inviting new members and fostering engagement.

Need Our Support?

If you're a ministry leader or need media assistance for a church-related activity, please reach out to us!
