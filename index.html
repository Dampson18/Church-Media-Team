Our Mission

The Media Team at Praise Congregation Oyibi is dedicated to leveraging technology to amplify the message of Christ. We support all ministries in reaching the congregation and beyond, ensuring a vibrant and engaging spiritual experience for all.

Spreading the Word

Utilizing various digital platforms to disseminate sermons, teachings, and biblical insights.

Enhancing Worship

Providing technical support for worship services, including sound, visuals, and live streaming.

Community Engagement

Connecting with our church family and the wider community through engaging online content and interactions.
