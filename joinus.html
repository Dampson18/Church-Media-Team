Join Our Team

Serve God and the church through media ministry.

Why Serve with The Media Team?

The Media Team plays a vital role in enabling Praise Congregation Oyibi to fulfill its mission. By joining us, you become an essential part of sharing the Gospel, enhancing our worship services, and connecting with our community both locally and globally. It's a unique opportunity to:

  • Utilize your skills: Whether you're tech-savvy or eager to learn, there's a place for you to contribute your talents.
  • Grow spiritually: Serving in ministry deepens your faith and understanding of God's work.
  • Build community: Connect with fellow believers who share a passion for service and media.
  • Make an impact: Directly contribute to the spread of God's Word and the vibrancy of our church.

No prior experience? No problem! We believe in equipping and training our team members. What's most important is a heart for service and a willingness to learn.

Roles We're Looking For

We have a variety of volunteer positions available. Explore where your gifts can best serve:

Camera Operator

Help capture our services and events with a keen eye, ensuring engaging visuals for our broadcasts.

Sound Technician Assistant

Assist with microphone setup, sound mixing, and ensuring clear audio for both live and streamed services.

Projectionist/Visuals Specialist

Manage sermon slides, song lyrics, and announcements on screen during services.

Video Editor

Help edit recorded sermons, testimonies, and promotional videos for online platforms.

Graphic Designer

Create compelling visual content for social media, website, and event promotions.

Social Media Manager

Help schedule posts, engage with our online community, and share church content across platforms.

Website Content Contributor

Assist with updating website content, event listings, and sermon archives.

Technical Support Assistant

Provide general tech support, troubleshoot equipment, and assist with IT needs.
